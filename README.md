# 🎛️ Applet Library

A collection of interactive web applets for mathematics and science education.

---

## Applets

### 🧲 [Forces on a Ramp](https://ohiomathteacher.github.io/applet-library/forces-on-ramp/)
Interactive physics simulation for exploring forces on inclined planes. Visualize force decomposition, friction effects, and energy conservation with draggable objects and real-time physics.

### 🍬 [Integer Partitioning](https://ohiomathteacher.github.io/applet-library/integer-partitioning/)
Explore integer partitions through jelly bean distribution, Ferrers diagrams, and constraint filters.

### 🎱 [Momentum & Collisions](https://ohiomathteacher.github.io/applet-library/momentum-collisions/)
Interactive billiard ball physics demonstrating conservation of momentum in elastic collisions. Discovery learning through direct manipulation. Companion to Forces on a Ramp - both built from McKenzie Sorce's field observations.

### 🧭 [Reference Angles v2](https://ohiomathteacher.github.io/applet-library/reference-angles/)
Enhanced unit circle with three new features: **Predict Signs Mode** (purple button - predict +/− for sin/cos/tan before revealing), **Exact Values** for special angles (0°/30°/45°/60°/90° show fractions/radicals), and **All Quadrants view** (see reference triangles in all four quadrants simultaneously with color-coded arcs). Built from field-lesson student data.

### 💰 [Mortgage Payment Visualizer](https://ohiomathteacher.github.io/applet-library/mortgage-visualizer/mortgage-visualizer.html)
Watch the principal-vs-interest split on a monthly mortgage payment over the full life of the loan. See how slowly the balance drops in early years, and how the ratio flips over time. Built from Savannah Strack's compound interest field lesson feedback.

---

## About

These applets are designed for hands-on mathematical exploration in the classroom. Each applet is self-contained with its own README and can be used independently.
