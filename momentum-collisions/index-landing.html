🎓 Why Two Versions?

Comparing these versions shows students and educators the power of thoughtful design. Version 1 is a solid physics simulation. Version 2 transforms that simulation into a complete inquiry-based learning tool with:

  • Guided challenges that scaffold student thinking
  • Adaptive difficulty levels for different learners
  • Multi-language support for diverse classrooms
  • A digital lab notebook for capturing and reflecting on data

This is what's possible when we design for learning, not just demonstration.
